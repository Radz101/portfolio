About:

ABC company (fictional company) is a leading regional supermarket, offering high quality products and personilized services. Due to increasing competition, customers are switching to online grocery shoppimg. ABC plans to add value by finding a way to leverage its successful premium store model and replicate it into a differentiating digital experience.

Duration: 3 weeks
Categories: UX Research, User persona, Ideation, Prototyping
Tools: Sketch, invision, miro, trello
