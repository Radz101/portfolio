Delifresh

An e-commerce project case study

Context:

Delifresh is a project I completed for Ironhack’s UX/UI course that I participated in from March 2019 to June 2019. Over the course, I learned and applied detailed processes and methods of UX design and research to improve my skill set as a designer and researcher.


About Delifresh:

It is a leading regional supermarket, offering high quality products and personilized services. They strive to create a “neighborhood shopping environment” to stand out from their competition.


Role: UX & UI Designer
Duration: 4 weeks
Categories: UX Research, User Persona, Ideation, Prototyping
Tools: Sketch, Invision, Miro, Trello


The challenge:

Delifresh's vision is to design a user experience that goes above and beyond the e-commerce marketplace models that other competitors are offering. They want to add value to their customers by creating a differentiating digital experience to help customers minimize their planning and maximize their time.

Key takeaways

  • Communicate effectively with your team members and stakeholders.
  • Be curious and always ask questions.
  • Ask for design feedback at an early stage of your process.
  • Always test your design! Unfortunately I was not able to conduct usability testing for this project as it was out of the scope. But as a designer, I’d love to observe users actually engaging with the product to identify pains and gains.
