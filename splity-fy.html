Splity

A task manager app case study

Context:

Splity is a project I worked on during Ironhackâ€™s UX/UI Course that I participated in from March 2019 to June 2019. The idea behind creating this app was to help people manage their travel related tasks when they are travelling in a group. The entire project was completed in just 3 weeks of time from idea generation to final prototype.

Role: UX & UI Designer
Duration: 3 weeks
Categories: UX Research, Information Architecture, Prototyping
Tools: Figma, Sketch, Principle, Invision


Design phases


People problem statement:

"Managing and co-ordinating tasks while travelling in a group is tough"


Research:

To start the project with, I created an online survey to gain a basic understanding of what the goals, behaviours, likes and dislikes of the users are when it comes to travelling in a group. I also conducted depth interviews to dig deeper into user goals and habits related to traveling with friends. The aim was to understand user needs and pain points better.


Analyse:

After gathering different types of data during research phase, it was time to start making sense of all different forms of results. I created user persona and affinity daigram.
