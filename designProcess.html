Engage and empathize with users:

In order to gain a greater understanding of the end-users and the problems they face, there is no better way than by talking directly to them. I use qualitative and quantitative methods to gather insights, such as interviews, surveys (to understand user behaviors) qualitative user research reports, personas, data summaries and design ideas.


Explore the design space

Having a good understanding of the end-user's pain points and their work environment, I uncover design opportunities to improve their life. I apply creative techniques to brainstorm on new solutions, create storyboards, define use cases, as well as sketch design ideas and wireframes.


Iterate and validate design solutions

I collect feedbacks and conduct usability tests to improve and refine the design solution. I use physical paper or Balsamiq to create static prototypes. I also build interactive High-Fi prototypes with Figma, Sketch, Invision or Loom. Based on the findings I make improvements until I find a design solution that is bound to be adopted and embraced.


Document design solutions

I work closely with various development teams to implement my designs. I create interaction flows and formulate design guidelines.
