Nidaba

Online marketplace for organizing and joining workshops

This case study is a work in progress

Context:

I participated in a 10 weeks project collaboration with company How I Can. How I Can is a team of young designers and developers that supports female founders in building a functional prototype within a strict timeline of 10 weeks. During this time, I helped a female founder in re-designing here website 'Nidaba' along with another product designer from March'20 - June'20.


About Nidaba:

It is an online marketplace for organizing and joining workshops based in Jordan.


Role: UX & UI Designer
Duration: 10 weeks
Categories: UX Research, User Persona, Ideation, Designing, Prototyping
Tools: Figma, Loom, Miro, Trello


Goals:

1. Create a smooth user expereince for instructors to post details of their workshop.
2. Create a communication forum for effective student-instructor communication.


The process


User persona


Ideation

Once we better understood our idea and explored our datasets, we began additional ideation for our vision. We quickly settled on the basic components and layout of our visualization through several quick rounds of sketches and mid-fidelity wireframes. We narrowed our scope to focus on Instructors user expereince and explored interactions around posting a workshop, how we could display profile, workshop details, and design a comunication forum that will allow instructors and students to communicate effectively.
