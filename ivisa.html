About iVisa:

iVisa is a travel document company. It help users with travel document services like e-visas, passport renewals, passport photos etc.

Role: UX/UI Designer
Duration: Since 2020
Categories: UX Research, Design systems, Prototyping, User testing, Data collation
Tools: Figma, AdobeXD, Maze, Whimsical, Clickup, Slack, Zeplin


Key highlights of my role:

  • I am in charge of conducting research for travel documents team. I conduct user testing both moderated and un-moderated using Maze and Userinterviews.com platform
  • I also conduct many design thinking workshops with stakeholders and key project owners
  • After joining iVisa I became more efficient in my UI skills and thus created design systems in figma to increase design efficiency
